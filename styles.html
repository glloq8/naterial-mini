Affirmed styles

At Naterial we are always looking for simplicity to define uncluttered, tidy and easy-going spaces. But also, with the desire of refined and personalized decoration. We provide an answer to different atmospheres to match different outdoor spaces, landscapes and architectures.

Contemporary

The contemporary style highlights graphic and minimal materials (wood, stone and coated aluminium). The metal is associated to soft fabrics, wood and concrete effects. The colour mood is not only natural but incorporates many green and grey shades (olive greens, dark and light greys). It can be enriched with colourful tones to bring life and vitality. This style is characterized by functionality and clean lines to simplify your life. Geometrical structures are very important to create and compose the general aesthetic for volumes and shapes.

Natural

The natural style is treated with a sensual minimalism. It is a soft mood with vegetal influences using a pastel shades colour palette of warm earthy neutrals. Especially with atonally beige, green tones and slightly greyish. Different materials (wood, metals, fabrics, rattan, natural fibers and ceramics) can be used but always with a touch of softness to bring an easy atmosphere. Shapes, hand-waving and finishings bring the balance between the rhythm and the harmony. There is a desire for simplicity and peacefulness with no-fuss and no glitz.

Traditional

The traditional style expresses the juxtaposition of modernity and historicity to create an atmosphere of authenticity. It reflects the desire to furnish a classical decoration with simplicity and elegance to bring an impression of revival. This style mixes materials like marble, metal and hand-weaved rattan. The colours experiments pastel shades and grey tones (from the white to the dark grey). All of it to create a sophisticated mood.
